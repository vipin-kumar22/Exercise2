OVERVIEW

Heliot Gym Company is a fitness organization known for its innovative approach to health and wellness. They focus on providing high-quality fitness services, including state-of-the-art gym facilities, personalized training programs, and wellness initiatives.

Julius Juul and Victor Juul are the founders of the Danish fashion brand HELIOT EMIL, launched in 2017. Julius, the Creative Director, drives the brand's avant-garde, futuristic designs and innovative use of materials, often exploring themes of contrast and deconstruction. Victor, as CEO, manages the business operations, ensuring the brand's vision aligns with commercial success. .
